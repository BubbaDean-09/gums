How to Save Your Progress

to save your progress, you need to progress far enough to get the save tab, and then click save on slot 1. When the site gets blocked your progress WILL reset. To help avoid this, press the "get the current game as text" and then save this in a google doc or something, you will have to do this multiple times as you progress through the game. Then when you want to load your save again, you progress far enough to unlock the save tab, scroll all the way down to text load, and paste the text you had saved in your doc.
